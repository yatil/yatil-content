Just quickly announcing [AccessifyHTML5.js](https://github.com/yatil/accessifyhtml5.js) is now online in version 1.0. Biggest feature: [Documentation!](https://github.com/yatil/accessifyhtml5.js#readme) ;-) You’re now able to add role=“main” to your main content and the script stops changing your HTML role attributes if there are some present.
